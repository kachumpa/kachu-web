Designing for Community Empowerment: Deafness in the Nilgiris

This project was part of my Master's Dissertation at NIFT, Mumbai. It was a design-based investigation into Deaf and Hearing interactions with a focus on the rural trival community of the Nilgiris in Tamil Nadu with the help of Aswini Hospital, Gudalur. Guided by participatory action research apporach and traditional design process where problem-solving emerges directly from people's needs, the study investigates the role of communication design tools in social development.
