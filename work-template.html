Raho Safe

Pee Safe's Organic Period Care Range caters to the modern woman looking for eco-friendly and safe menstrual products. It was important to have a unified visual language to distinguish it from the regular range of products.
The keywords were natural, organic, biodegradable, recyclable.
Every single packaging was made with the least amount of negative environmental impact. 100% recycled paper was used for the monocartons, informational inserts and envelopes.
Dealing with fairly newer period products to the market, it was essential to get the right information to potential customers especially around tampons and menstrual cups. Inserts were carefully designed to be a friendly guide to challenge the usual warning like booklets that come with such products.
