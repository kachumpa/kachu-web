The Brand

Raho Safe was incepted to create economical yet effective products that help fight germs and bacteria. Personal hygiene and wellness is a human right and should be afforded by all. Sanitation is no more a privilege, a want or a desire. It is now the need of the hour that can't be taken for granted. Raho Safe's mission lies within the realms of protect, prevent and care. From beginning to end, Raho Safe guarantees your safety.

Protect, Prevent and Care.

Products

Instead of adapting, the only solution is adapting better. Raho Safe helps us to do that. The products include a wide range of sanitizers and protectants that help keep your surroundings disinfected to avoid any contamination. It's a one stop solution to all your safety essentials, ranging from medically approved disinfectants and sanitisers, faces masks and face guards, and accessible sanitary pads.
