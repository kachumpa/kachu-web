All About Cups

Concept

Menstrual cups were a fairly new addition to the period hygiene space owing to the fact that many women constantly confused about choices. Issues noticed through research - viriginity, fear, permissions. Used social media to break that barrier. Meaningful communication and personalised answers to help steer people to the right choice.
